What is ParkSmart?

ParkSmart is a smart parking discovery and reservation platform that connects drivers with available parking spaces in real time. Whether you're commuting to work, visiting a mall, or heading to an event, ParkSmart ensures you have a guaranteed spot waiting for you.

Discover

Search thousands of public and private parking spaces near you with real-time availability.

Reserve

Book your slot in seconds with our instant reservation system. Your spot is held just for you.

Pay

Use your ParkSmart wallet for seamless, cashless payments with full transaction history.

Host

Own a parking space? List it on ParkSmart and earn passive income from unused spots.
